More Features, More Benefits

We've got your needs and wants covered.

Instantly Balanced Classlists

Classlist Gener8r's "SmartList" algorithm uses the data from survey's to generate classes balanced by class size, gender, behavior, academics, communication, and social dynamics.

Specific Individual Flexibility

This allows for individual students to be manually placed in certain classes

Easy Upload and Distribution

Our tailored Excel template integration allows for ease of upload and sharing of classes among staff

Secure Student Information

Hosted in the cloud, no student data is saved in effort to protect the privacy of students and teachers

User Friendly

Developed with constant feedback from teachers in effort maximize user experience

Save time

Our lightweight application allows teachers to allocate their precious time elsewhere

Academic Rubric

{#
#}

1 = Identified good chance, will qualify
2 = High achieving, above grade level, independent, minimal assistance
3 = At grade level, generally average student
4 = Below grade level, needs assistance from teacher, can be a SPED student who is in the classroom for most subjects
5 = Far below grade level, SPED

Behavior Rubric

{#
#}

1 = Highly motivated, no behavioral issues
2 = Dependable, infrequent re-direction
3 = Frequent re-direction needed
4 = Has significant behavior needs
5 = High needs student, has para support in the classroom

Communication Rubric

{#
#}

1 = Routine parent communication needed
2 = Periodic communication needed because of special requests/issues regarding their child
3 = Communication 3x per month or more or administrative assistance needed with communication

Three Simple Steps

You have the data, we have the algorithm

Directions:

1. One teacher representative in your grade level is responsible for uploading a completed template.

2. The completed template should include information about every student moving to the next grade level.
     Pro Tip: Have each teacher download and fill out this template, and have the teacher representative merge these and upload.

3. After uploading and submitting, your generated classlists will be available in an easily downloadable and shareable Excel spreadsheet.
