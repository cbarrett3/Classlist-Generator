Three Simple Steps

You have the data, we have the algorithm

Directions:


1) Each teacher in your grade-level should fill out the Excel template with information about every one of their students that is moving to the next grade level.

     Note: Teachers can either download the template individually, or it can be shared to them.



2) One "teacher representative" is responsible for merging all templates within the grade level, and uploading a completed template.

     Note: Teachers will have to send their completed templates to the "teacher representative".



3) Immediately after uploading and submitting, your generated classlists will be emailed to the teacher representative in an easily downloadable and shareable Excel spreadsheet.
